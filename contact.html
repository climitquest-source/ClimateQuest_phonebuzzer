FAQ & Contact

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cli‑Mit Quest?

Cli‑Mit Quest is an educational quiz game that uses a Jeopardy‑style board to explore topics such as waste management, climate emergencies, community gardening, sustainability and food security.

How do phone buzzers work?

When the host enables phone buzzers on the Play page, a unique room code and QR code are generated. Players navigate to the join link on their phones, enter the code, choose their team and press BUZZ when ready.

What if we don’t have phones?

You can use printed coloured cards instead. Hold up the card when your team knows the answer; the host will select your team manually in the question modal.

Can I play solo?

Yes. Choose “1 Team” during setup and play through the board on your own. The question timer still adds a fun challenge!

How long does each question last?

Each question has a 30‑second timer. If no team answers correctly within the time limit, the question closes automatically.
