What is Cli‑Mit Quest?

Cli‑Mit Quest is a Jeopardy‑style game designed to educate and engage players on topics like waste management, climate emergencies, community gardening, sustainability and food security. Perfect for workshops, classrooms and social gatherings, it combines friendly competition with practical knowledge.

Features

Play Together or Solo

Set up 1–4 teams or play solo. Each game adapts to your group size and pace.

Phone Buzzers

Let players buzz in using their phones – or use colourful cards as a simple backup.

Informative Questions

Every question is paired with a brief explanation so you learn the why as well as the what.

Post‑Game Summary

See final scores and relive the game with a summary screen at the end.

Play Offline (Physical Kit)

Prefer a hands‑on experience? Visit the Physical Kit page to download everything you need for offline play – cards, buzzers and an answer key – and enjoy Cli‑Mit Quest without a screen.
