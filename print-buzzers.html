Printable Buzzers

Below are four postcard‑sized buzzers. Print them on paper or cardstock and cut them out. Each card displays a solid colour with the word “BUZZ” in the centre and the team name underneath. During the game, teams hold up their coloured buzzer; the host clicks the matching colour button to register who buzzed first.
